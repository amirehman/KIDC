Define & Mission

What is the definition of Dubai Police Knowledge, Innovation and Development Center?

The Knowledge, Innovation, and Development Center is a specialized unit within Dubai Police, responsible for driving knowledge and innovation initiatives using the latest technologies in the security sector. Our team is composed of highly skilled professionals equipped with the necessary competencies in the field of security services and is motivated to adopt the latest modern technologies to transform the current reality and anticipate future challenges. Our goal is to establish Dubai Police as a global leader in knowledge, innovation and police development.
